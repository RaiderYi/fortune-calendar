# Editorial East Roadmap

> Project: MysticEast  
> Scope: Marketing website evolution for English-speaking audiences  
> Version: 1.0  
> Last Updated: 2026-04-11

---

## 1. Strategic Goal

Build MysticEast into an **editorial-style cultural platform** that:

- expresses Eastern philosophy with premium visual storytelling,
- continuously publishes image-led cultural products and narratives,
- captures organic traffic through SEO and content depth,
- converts visitors into free reading users and email subscribers.

Core growth path:

`Landing -> Calculator -> Result -> Subscribe -> Return via content`

---

## 2. Positioning

### Brand Position

MysticEast is not a generic fortune tool. It is a **cultural reading studio**:

- Eastern philosophical depth,
- modern English clarity,
- practical reflection for everyday decisions.

### Audience (Primary)

- English-speaking users (US/UK/CA/AU),
- interested in wellness, self-discovery, cultural meaning,
- prefers high-trust and premium editorial experiences over noisy marketing pages.

---

## 3. Editorial East Design System

### Visual Direction

- **Magazine-like layout**: asymmetric blocks, hero spread, story-led sections.
- **Eastern restraint**: whitespace, rhythm, soft hierarchy, no visual shouting.
- **Material language**: ink-wash atmosphere, paper-like panels, seal accents.

### Color and Tone

- Primary: deep indigo (`#2D1B4E`) for depth and trust.
- Accent: muted gold (`#C9A227`) for premium cultural signal.
- Support: jade (`#00A86B`) for vitality and renewal.
- Surface: warm cream (`#F5F1E8`) for editorial calm.

### Typography

- Display: elegant serif for narrative and cultural authority.
- Body: highly readable sans for modern usability.
- Strong rhythm through line-height, spacing cadence, and short paragraph blocks.

---

## 4. Information Architecture (Near-Term)

### Core Layers

1. **Editorial Home**: philosophy + featured spreads + monthly theme.
2. **Free Reading Path**: calculator and result conversion loop.
3. **Collections**: image-led cultural products and service stories.
4. **Journal**: SEO article engine and long-form educational content.

### Navigation Intent

- Keep primary navigation concise and publication-like.
- Push secondary links to footer and contextual section links.
- Preserve a single clear entry CTA: free reading.

---

## 5. Homepage Editorial Framework

Homepage structure target:

1. **Hero Spread** (philosophy + trust + primary action)
2. **How It Works** (3-step clarity)
3. **Applied Philosophy** (feature blocks)
4. **Editorial East Collection** (image-forward product cards)
5. **Featured Spread** (horizontal cover layout)
6. **Monthly Theme Slot** (evergreen update module)
7. **Social Proof** (quality over quantity)
8. **Final CTA** (calm, high-intent conversion)

---

## 6. Content and Publishing Model

### Monthly Operating Cycle

- Month theme (one narrative umbrella),
- 1 featured editorial spread,
- 3-5 related products/services cards refresh,
- 2-4 SEO blog articles,
- 1 monthly cultural story update.

### Content Types

- Long-form interpretation articles,
- practical “how to apply” pieces,
- visual explainers for Five Elements / BaZi concepts,
- product-backed cultural narratives.

### Editorial Quality Rules

- Explain, do not mystify.
- Cultural respect over exotic decoration.
- Practical next step in every piece.
- Consistent tone: calm, intelligent, grounded.

---

## 7. SEO Roadmap

### Technical SEO Baseline

- Canonicals for marketing pages,
- complete metadata and OG/Twitter cards,
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`,
- structured data (`Organization`, `WebSite`, `CollectionPage`, page-level entities).

### Content SEO

- Pillar clusters:
  - BaZi basics,
  - Five Elements in modern life,
  - timing and decision frameworks,
  - cultural interpretation and case-based storytelling.
- Internal link mesh:
  - Home <-> Collections <-> Blog <-> Calculator.
- Search-intent writing:
  - beginner education,
  - practical application,
  - comparison and myth-busting.

### SEO KPI Targets (90 Days)

- +30% indexed page impressions,
- +25% organic sessions,
- +20% calculator starts from organic traffic,
- +15% subscription conversion from result page.

---

## 8. Conversion and Trust Strategy

### Trust Signals

- privacy-first language,
- cultural authenticity framing,
- high-quality testimonials and proof points,
- clear “what you get” before asking for commitment.

### Conversion Principles

- reduce friction (no forced signup at entry),
- reward curiosity quickly (instant free insight),
- deepen engagement via story-led follow-ups,
- maintain one obvious next step per section.

---

## 9. 8-Week Execution Plan

### Phase A (Weeks 1-2): Editorial Shell

- finalize header/footer publication style,
- lock section spacing and typographic rhythm,
- normalize visual tokens and panel patterns.

### Phase B (Weeks 3-4): Collection and Story Engine

- expand product image system,
- standardize horizontal feature spread template,
- operationalize monthly theme slot updates.

### Phase C (Weeks 5-6): SEO Depth

- add article-level structured data,
- improve topic clusters and internal links,
- optimize metadata and content search alignment.

### Phase D (Weeks 7-8): Conversion Refinement

- test copy hierarchy on hero/result pages,
- optimize subscription microcopy,
- tighten calculator -> result -> subscribe flow.

---

## 10. Governance and Quality Bar

Before publishing any monthly update:

- Design check: editorial consistency and visual restraint.
- Content check: clarity, usefulness, and cultural respect.
- SEO check: metadata, links, structured data, crawlability.
- UX check: mobile readability and CTA clarity.
- Performance check: image weight and load stability.

---

## 11. Definition of Success

MysticEast should feel like:

- a premium cultural journal, not a template marketing site,
- a practical guide, not abstract mysticism,
- a growing visual publication that can continuously ship new image-led products and stories.
