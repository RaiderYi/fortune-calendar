# MysticEast - Claude Project Rules

## Superpower Skills Framework (REQUIRED)

This project follows the **Superpower Skills** methodology. Every task must apply:

### Copywriting Superpower
Transform complex Eastern metaphysics into accessible Western wellness concepts:
- ❌ "Your BaZi chart reveals your destiny"
- ✅ "Your Elemental Blueprint shows your natural patterns"
- ❌ "Day Master analysis"
- ✅ "Discover your Core Element"

### Visual Design Superpower
Maintain the Fusion aesthetic:
- Deep indigo backgrounds (#2D1B4E)
- Gold accents for premium feel (#C9A227)
- Jade for wellness/CTAs (#00A86B)
- Cream backgrounds (#F5F1E8)
- Crystal card effects with subtle shadows

### UX Strategy Superpower
- Free calculator is the entry point
- Every page leads to either:
  1. Try free calculator
  2. Email bazirili@foxmail.com
- No pricing during beta
- Build trust before asking for commitment

### Technical Superpower
- Next.js 14 App Router
- TypeScript strict
- Tailwind with custom design tokens
- Framer Motion for smooth interactions
